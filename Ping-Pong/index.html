Ping-Pong

Let's play!

This website is called Ping-pong, which will be generating an ouput of numbers. It's a funway of teaching kids maths. It has been proved that, teaching children under the theme of "education for fun" has had effective reults.

Rules:

It takes a number from a user and returns a range of numbers from 1 to the chosen number with the following exceptions:

  • Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "ping"
  • Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with "pong"
  • Numbers divisible by 15 are replaced with "pingpong"
